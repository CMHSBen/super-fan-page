Valorant

 Valorant is n online tactical first person shooter game made by the company Riot Games. Set in the near future, it features a 5 v 5 gamemode with 9 different maps where one side is the attacking team and the other side is the defending team. There are 21 agents to choose from, each having their own unique set of abilities. The goal of the attackers is to plant a device called the spike and defend it while it does it's job, and the defender's job is to stop them from planting the spike or deactivate it before it goes off.
